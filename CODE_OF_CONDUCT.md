# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and provide actionable feedback.
- Focus on ideas and code, not individuals.
- Help newcomers and keep discussions professional.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or intimidation.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Any conduct inappropriate in a professional community.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and other community channels.
